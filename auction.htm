Take home a piece of evolution!
When:  October 18, 2003
Where: The New Pittsburgh Convention Center
Time:   6 p.m. - 2 a.m.

In mid-September, sponsors will donate their dinosaurs back to DinoMite Days for auction. These dinosaurs will join several other pre-selected dinosaurs that will be auctioned on October 18, 2003.

Sponsors will select a beneficiary to receive a percentage of the proceeds from the donated dinosaurs. The remaining proceeds will benefit Carnegie Museum of Natural History's public and educational programs, which reach almost one million people throughout the region.

The price to attend the entire Gala evening, including the dinner and the Dinosaur Auction, is $200 per person. You may choose instead to arrive after the Dinner & Auction for Jurassic Jungle, the entertainment portion of the evening beginning at 10 PM, for $40 per person ($50 at the door). Details will be available in the invitation, which will be sent in late September.

Check this page for more information as the Gala and Auction draw near!  If you'd like to be mailed an invitation to purchase tickets, write to us at GalaGuestList@CarnegieMuseums.org.
