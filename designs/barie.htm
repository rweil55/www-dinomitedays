About the Design
Barie's Stegosaurus is designed with a combination of mixed media, painting, and printing methods in the style of her current work, with colorful geometric shapes and floral patterns. "Prehistoric Printasaurus" is covered with a variety of designs employing a botanical garden motif. Color is the principal and thematic motif that threads the multiple images together.
