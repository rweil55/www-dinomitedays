About the Design
Who can resist a giant piggy bank? "Dolly Dollarsaurus" and "Bill Dollarsaurus" are matching dinos brought to life for sponsor Dollar Bank by Dymun + Company, one of Pittsburgh’s most creative advertising and public relations agencies. One "Dollarsaurus" dinosaur is being auctioned in this online offering, while the other will be auctioned live on October 18. Interest is intense.

You can relive your childhood by giving a home to this pink perfection of a dino. Giant dollar bill included!

About the Sponsor
Since 1855 Dollar Bank has invested billions of dollars in millions of lives and thousands of businesses across the region. In an age of mega mergers in the banking industry, Dollar Bank has bucked the trend, remaining independent and committed to the people and businesses of southwestern Pennsylvania and northeastern Ohio.
