About the Design
This Stegosaurus knows that he represents his school to the larger community. With school blazer neatly pressed and backpack at the ready, he stands tall and looks everyone in the eye. "SEAWolfasaurus" proudly welcomes visitors to St. Edmund’s Academy in Squirrel Hill. Standing sentry at the Forbes Avenue entrance, this dino is a product of the imagination of many children and one school’s commitment to seeing their vision from design to finished piece of art.

Children from both the Lower School and Upper School helped create SEAWolfasaurus, whose name is a combination of the school’s initials and its mascot, the wolf. The dino skin is personalized with students’ handprints.

About the School
St. Edmund’s Academy is an independent, coeducational school, PreKindergarten through Grade Eight. Educational excellence is achieved through a traditional curriculum augmented by innovative teaching. Students leave St. Edmund’s well prepared to encounter the challenges of high school with confidence, as well-adjusted citizens and contributors to the community.
