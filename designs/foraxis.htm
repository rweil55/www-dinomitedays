Theme: A possible reaction to the paradox between the ancient and modern worlds
 
 

About the Design
Don Lee, team leader: "We are trying to show the aggressive paradox between the modern world and the ancient one, and the possible reaction of such an encounter. The fiberglass body is cut into pieces and each part is then filled on the inside with broken-off electronic parts and covered with transparent poly resin. The parts are then suspended in mid-air by the means of spear-ended tubes, which at the bottom part are inserted in blocks of buried concrete."

About the Artists
FORAXIS is made up of four creative minds, specializing in many diverse areas of Industrial Design. We work together as a team on every project, communicating our ideas to each other until we have a winning concept. We also strive to communicate effectively with our client throughout all stages of the design process.Our passion for good design drives us to create fresh, clever, thought-provoking functional objects that interact with human emotions.
