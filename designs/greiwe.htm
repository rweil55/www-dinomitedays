  About the Design
Thousands of individual mirrors reflect the world around this Torosaurus. As viewers catch a glimpse of their reflections, they will be captured within the space of the dinosaur — instantly becoming a "still life" themselves.

 

About the Sponsors
Founded in 1992, The Mosites Company is a diversified real estate development and property management firm. Its mission is to create lasting value through well-designed projects with quality building materials and an emphasis on vibrant urban place making through public and private partnership.

Whole Foods Market, the world’s largest natural and organic foods supermarket, was founded in 1980 in Austin, Texas. The motto, “Whole Foods, Whole People, Whole Planet” captures the company’s mission to find success in customer satisfaction and wellness, employee excellence and happiness, enhanced shareholder value, community support, and environmental improvement.
