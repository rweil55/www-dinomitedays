Sponsored by: Carnegie Museum of Natural History
Fossil Location: Schenley Plaza, University of Pittsburgh Campus, Oakland
Auction Price: $1000
Materials: Metal tubing, Lexan®, aluminum, plexiglass
Theme: Sculptural representation of the reconstruction of fossil elements
 
 

About the Design
Fascinated by paleontology? Attracted to abstract art? "Rubix-O-Saurus" is your kind of dinosaur. The viewer stands transfixed, mentally reconstructing a Tyrannosaurus rex from the pieces.

The frame is constructed of extruded aluminum tubing (1 inch sq.) and mig welded. The cover panels are 3/8" clear Lexan® fastened with 3/8"x1" stainless steel screws and sealed with a latex caulking. Overall size is 96"x32" (each cube being 32 inches square). There are 3/8" aluminum angles brackets welded to the base for mounting. This Rubik’s Cube puzzle of a dinosaur will provide years of fascination.
