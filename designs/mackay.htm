  About the Design
A play on opposites was MacKay's goal with this design. Like a giant Fabergé egg, this bejeweled colossal is fascinating to behold. The jewels in this design are created through the use of mosaic. MacKay painted a majority of the design, and then added highly detailed mosaic sections. The mosaic work was done in stained glass and colored mirror. MacKay chose to work on a Stegosaurus because the plates running down its back create individual spaces within which to work. Each of these plates has a mosaic section. Colored mirror allows the piece to be constantly reflecting the changing light of day, which brings movement and a sparkle of life into the design.
