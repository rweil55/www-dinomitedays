About the Design
"PPG TyranAQUARIUM Rex" illustrates the residents of Pittsburgh that most of us have never met. Many forms of aquatic life are brightly painted on this cheerful dino, letting us know that our three beloved rivers offer more than just a pretty view and a place to water-ski; they are also home to millions of Pittsburghers that we will never see. This dinosaur would love to live in a garden, but she’d be most happy around—you guessed it—water!

About the Sponsor
PPG Industries is an $8 billion global manufacturer of coatings, glass, fiber glass and chemical products. The company has more than 120 major facilities in 23 countries around the world and is headquartered in Pittsburgh. PPG was founded near Pittsburgh in 1883 and is proud to support the region through programs like DinoMite Days.
