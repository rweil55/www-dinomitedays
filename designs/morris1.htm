About the Design
American icons decorate two of the three Burton Morris DinoMite Days dinosaurs. "I Want You" gives us a fresh, colorful look at Uncle Sam. With his flowing white hair and bright blue frock, this figure of Americana seems affable rather than imposing. The artist once again gives us a delightful spin on a common popular image.

From signature bottles for Perrier to commissions for Absolut Vodka and other companies, Burton Morris celebrates popular culture. The television sitcom "Friends" uses his works on-camera. Collectors are said to include Brad Pitt and John Travolta. Now you, too, can own a one-of-a-kind pop piece by this renowned artist.
