About the Design
Bridget Parris: "Celebrating the 'whimsical nature of nature' is the theme of my dinosaur structure for Carnegie Museum of Natural History's Dinomite Days.

"My design celebrates life in the natural world: A young man and his female suitor climb a lush, verdant tree. Birds flit by, as the setting sun casts a golden-orange glow to the entire scene." 

Greens and yellows of a woodland scene dominate the dinosaur from the front. The images grow across its back.
