About the Design
This is a must-see dinosaur. Visit the USX Tower “T” Station to see a Stegosaurus with connections. A video portal in the belly of "Renaissanceosaurus" allows this dino to show you her adventures around the city visiting other DinoMite Days dinosaurs. (She is such a natural interviewer, no wonder her friends call her Dino Shore!)

With her sleek, shiny, holographic skin, "Renaissanceosaurus" serves as a reflection of Port Authority’s commitment to our city’s future. Credit the Port Authority Creative Department with giving us one well-traveled dinosaur.

About the Sponsor
Port Authority of Allegheny County is committed to Pittsburgh’s renaissance. Connecting people to life, Port Authority helps move the city through its evolution into the 21st Century.
