  About the Design
"Sign-O-Saurus" has three interpretations: On the surface, it is a humorous "motorist warning" to the complexity of the Pittsburgh roadscape. On a deeper level it represents the "beast" that raises its ugly head in people when we are confronted with too much information in too little time, as well as the "signage" symbolizing the fate of the dinosaurs.

 

About the Sponsor
The Dominion Foundation supports initiatives and nonprofit organizations that improve the communities in which Dominion, one of the nation’s largest producers of energy, provides energy service and has business interests.
