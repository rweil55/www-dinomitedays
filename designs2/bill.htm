A fresh design, which features multi-colored dinosaurs along with words like "joy," "play," and "belonging," was completed by Pittsburgh-based artists Max Gonzales and Shane Pilster.

About the Sponsor
Dollar Bank was the original sponsor of the pink colored dinosaur. The new look was the result of a partnership between the Shady Lane School, in front of which Dollysaurus is located, and DS Kinsel, co-founder of the Garfield-based arts hub BOOM Concepts.
