About the Design
Inspiration came in bits and pieces to young women of The Ellis School as they deftly fashioned this Stegosaurus out of ceramic dishes, then grouted each inch between the plates. They dubbed the mosaic masterpiece "Plato." Karen Kaighin, photography teacher at Ellis and Pittsburgh Filmmakers, coordinated the project along with art teachers from throughout the school.

About the Sponsor
The Ellis School is an urban, independent college-preparatory school and the only dedicated to the education of girls and young women in Western Pennsylvania. Each student is inspired to succeed by nurturing her spirit, challenging her intellect and encouraging her vision of the future. In turn, we expect each student to deliver her best, to serve her community and to embrace the diversity of the faculty and student body.
