About the Design
A fitting dinosaur for Pittsburgh, "Dinosurgeon" celebrates the city’s role as a world-renowned center for the medical arts. This Tyrannosaurus rex is wearing blue surgical scrubs, glasses, ID tag, and don't forget the stethoscope! All are permanently bonded to the dinosaur’s body. A giant in the field, he will always stand ready to operate. The University of Pittsburgh Medical Center’s design team, led by David Seaman, gives us a dinosaur for the medical record books.

About the Sponsor
University of Pittsburgh Medical Center (UPMC) is the premier healthcare system in Western Pennsylvania and one of the largest nonprofit healthcare systems in the country. UPMC consistently places among the nation’s top hospitals in U.S. News & World Report’s honor roll ranking. UPMC has an enormous commitment to Western Pennsylvania, generously donating charitable contributions and uncompensated care to the community each year.
