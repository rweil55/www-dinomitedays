Dollar Bank DinoMite DaysSM Family Day
Sunday, October 19, 2003

Over 8,000 people stomped on over to the David Lawrence Convention Center on October 19, 2003 to discover the DinoMite Days Dinosaurs gathered in their nesting habitat. The whole herd bid a final farewell as they made tracks to their new homes. Download a guide to the day's events! (170 Kb Adobe Acrobat file) 

• Last chance to see any dinosaurs you might have missed.
• Visitors encountered a world from over 200 million years ago with 10-foot-tall prehistoric friends and SCIENCE ON STAGE presentations
• The winning ticket for the Dinosaur Raffle was drawn
• Kids could journey through the jungle habitat and unearth clues to meet the challenge of the DINOMITE DISCOVERY scavenger hunt
• Activities were provided by such organizations as the Pittsburgh Zoo & PPG Aquarium, Carnegie Museum of Art, National Aviary, Pittsburgh Center for the Arts, Pittsburgh Children's Museum, Phipps Conservatory & Botanical Gardens, and many more!

About the Family Day Sponsor
Dollar Bank has remained independent and committed to the people and businesses of southwestern Pennsylvania and northeastern Ohio. Since 1855 Dollar Bank has invested billions of dollars in millions of lives and thousands of businesses across the region. The Regional Showcase presented by Dollar Bank at the new David L. Lawrence Convention Center illustrates our long-term commitment and dedication to the economic development of our region.

Where the Dinosaurs Roam

For a donation to Carnegie Museum of Natural History, everyone had a chance to own a dinosaur! The designs of the raffle-saurs were created by Alexander Overby and Greg Sandor, and the winning tickets were drawn on Family Day.
