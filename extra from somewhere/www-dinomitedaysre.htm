Just arrived!  The DinoMite Days book uses hundreds of full-color photographs to illustrate the very creative path the 100 DinoMite Days dinosaurs followed as they made their way through the streets of the Pittsburgh region and into the hearts of its residents.

In addition to depicting the DinoMite art exhibit, the book gives readers an opportunity to dig deeper into the mysterious world of dinosaur collecting.
