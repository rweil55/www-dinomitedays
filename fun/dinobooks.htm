Dinosaur Booklist


Here are some books that will help you in your study of dinosaurs. Books marked with a star are recommended for younger readers.

* Barton, Byron. Bones, Bones, Dinosaur Bones. New York: Thomas Y. Crowell, 1990.

* Brandenberg, Aliki. Digging Up Dinosaurs. New York: Thomas Y. Crowell, 1981.

* ______ . Dinosaur Bones. New York: Harper Collins Publishers, 1988.

* ______.Fossils Tell of Long Ago. New York: Harper Collins Publishers, 1990.

Dinosaurs and Their Living Relatives. London: The British Museum, 1986.

* Discover Dinosaurs at The Carnegie. Pittsburgh: Carnegie Museum of Natural History, 1996.
(This and many other books are available at the Carnegie Museum's Natural History Store.)

Dixon, Dougal. The Macmillan Illustrated Encyclopedia of Dinosaurs and Prehistoric Animals. New York: MacMillan Publishing Company, 1988.

* Horner, John, and James Gorman. Maia: A Dinosaur Grows Up. Philadelphia: Courage Books, 1989.

Horner, John R., and Don Lessem. The Complete T. Rex. New York: Simon and Schuster, 1993.

* Kurokawa, Mitsuhiro. Dinosaur Valley. San Francisco: Chronicle Books, 1992.

Lambert, David. The Ultimate Dinosaur Book. New York: Dorling Kindersley, 1993.

Lauber, Patricia. Dinosaurs Walked Here and Other Stories Fossils Tell. New York: Bradbury Press, 1987.

______. Living with Dinosaurs. New York: Bradbury Press, 1991.

McGinnis, Helen J. Carnegie's Dinosaurs. Pittsburgh: Carnegie Museum of Natural History, 1982.
(This and many other books are available at the Carnegie Museum's Natural History Store.)

* Most, Bernard. The Littlest Dinosaurs. San Diego: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, 1989.

* ______. A Dinosaur Named After Me. San Diego: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, 1991.

Norman, David. Dinosaur! New York: Prentice Hall, 1991.

______.The Prehistoric World of the Dinosaur. New York: Gallery Books, 1988.

Norman, David, and Angela Milner. Dinosaur. Eyewitness Book. New York: Alfred A. Knopf, 1989.

Sattler, Helen Roney. The New Illustrated Dinosaur Dictionary. New York: Lothrop, 1990.

Taylor, Paul. Fossil. Eyewitness Book. New York: Alfred A. Knopf, 1990.

Witfield, Philip. Macmillan's Children's Guide to Dinosaurs and Other Prehistoric Animals. New York: MacMillan Publishing Company, 1992.

* These books are recommended for younger readers.
