Belemnobatis sismondae
Ancient Banjo Fish
Fossil Size: 2 feet long
Diet: Carnivore (ate mollusks and other invertebrates)
Habitat: Aquatic
When: 163 Million Years Ago (this type of fish still exists!)
Skeletal Features: This bottom-dwelling fish had a flattened, diamond-shaped body and expanded front fins. It also had round, flat teeth for crushing mollusks and other invertebrates from the sea floor.
