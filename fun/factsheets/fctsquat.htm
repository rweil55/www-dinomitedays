Squatina alifera
Ancient Angel Shark
Fossil Size: 3 feet long
Diet: Carnivore
Habitat: Aquatic
When: 163 Million Years Ago (this type of fish still exists!)
Skeletal Features: This shark was a bottom-dweller, with a flat body and large side fins. Its eyes were on the top of its head, and it had round, flat teeth for crushing shelled animals. A modern form of the angel shark is very common in today's seas!
