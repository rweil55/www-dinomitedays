Xiphactinus audax
Extinct Bulldog Fish
Fossil Size:
Length: 14-16 feet
Diet: Carnivore
Habitat: Aquatic
When: 144-66 Million Years Ago
Skeletal Features: The gigantic carnivore, Xiphactinus, had long pointed teeth, which it used to catch other fish. One Xiphactinus skeleton fossil has in its belly, the skeleton of a 6 foot fish that it swallowed whole! What a lunch!
